Everything you need for a seamless visit to Doha for Qatar Education Expo 2026.

The Venue: Sheraton Grand Doha Resort & Convention Hotel

Experience the pinnacle of Qatari hospitality at the iconic event venue, located in the heart of the city.

  • Central Location: Situated on the scenic Corniche, offering stunning views of the Arabian Gulf and easy access to West Bay.
  • World-Class Facilities: A state-of-the-art convention center, multiple dining options, and luxury amenities.
  • Easy Accessibility: A short drive from Hamad International Airport and well-connected to local transport.

Visa & Entry Requirements

Qatar offers easy visa options for visitors from around the globe. Please check the requirements applicable to your nationality.

Hayya Platform

For many nationalities, the Hayya platform is the main gateway for entry visas. It offers a streamlined digital service for tourists and event attendees.

Visit Hayya Portal

Ministry of Interior Portal

Some nationalities may need to apply via the official Qatar Visa Portal. Check this source for detailed eligibility and application rules.

Visit MOI Portal

General Advice

We strongly advise all international visitors to confirm the latest visa regulations with their nearest Qatari embassy well in advance of their travel.
